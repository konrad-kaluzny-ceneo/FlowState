# S-52: Honest focus and break time totals

- **Outcome:** user sees in "Twój dzień" the total time actually spent on focus cycles and on breaks — including cycles stopped early, breaks stopped early, and cycles ended by finishing the task — instead of only cycles the timer carried to their end
- **Change ID:** focus-and-break-time-totals
- **Linear:** [FLO-105](https://linear.app/flowstate-10xdev/issue/FLO-105/user-sees-in-twoj-dzien-the-total-time-actually-spent-on-focus-cycles)
- **GitHub:** [#204](https://github.com/konrad-kaluzny-ceneo/FlowState/issues/204)
- **PRD refs:** US-07
- **Prerequisites:** S-24, S-30, S-42, S-45, S-50
- **Parallel with:** S-51 (independent); precedes S-48 (trends need honest totals first)
- **Blockers:** —
- **Acceptance:**
  - Daily totals count elapsed time from focus cycles the user stopped before the timer ran out
  - Daily totals count elapsed time from cycles that ended because the task was finished mid-cycle
  - Daily totals report total break time as its own figure, next to total focus time — today break time is not reported at all
  - A break stopped early counts the time actually spent on it
  - Paused time is not counted as focus or break time (S-24 pause semantics preserved — pause is not work)
  - A cycle that never started contributes nothing; timer accuracy and cycle cadence are unchanged
  - Both guest and authenticated modes report the same totals for the same day
- **Resolved decisions:**
  - **Forward-only** (user, 2026-07-14) — the new totals count from the change onward. Days already recorded keep the totals they were shown; no backfill or recompute of past cycles.
- **Unknowns:**
  - Are focus and break shown as two totals, or is break time folded into a single "time in session" figure? — owner: user. Block: no.
  - Does the per-hour distribution and work-type breakdown (Podsumowanie charts) also switch to elapsed time, or only the headline totals? — owner: user. Block: no.
  - Does the current 1-minute floor per counted cycle survive for very short partial cycles, or do sub-minute cycles round to zero? — owner: implementer. Block: no.
  - Does the light in-flow recap footprint (S-30/S-42) restate break time, or does break time live only in Podsumowanie? — owner: implementer. Block: no.
- **Risk:** `computeCycleFocusedMinutes` is the single shared reader — it returns 0 for anything that is not a COMPLETED WORK cycle, and both the in-flow recap (S-30/S-42) and the Podsumowanie KPIs/charts (S-45) read through it. Widening it silently changes every surface that consumes it, so the change must be made at that seam deliberately, with the pause exclusion held. Because totals are forward-only, a day that straddles the ship crosses two counting rules — the day of the change may read low or inconsistent, and that is accepted rather than patched.
- **Status:** ready
